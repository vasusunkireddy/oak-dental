5 Tips for Maintaining Oral Hygiene During Pregnancy

Pregnancy is a time of significant change, and maintaining oral health is crucial for both mother and baby. Here are five expert tips to keep your smile healthy:

  • Brush twice daily with a fluoride toothpaste to prevent cavities.
  • Floss daily to remove plaque and prevent gum disease.
  • Visit your dentist regularly for check-ups and cleanings.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in calcium and avoid sugary snacks.
  • Stay hydrated to combat dry mouth, which can increase the risk of tooth decay.

By following these tips, you can ensure your oral health remains a priority during this special time.

What to Expect During a Root Canal Procedure

A root canal treatment is a common procedure to save a damaged or infected tooth. Here’s what you can expect at OAK Dental Hospital:

  1. Diagnosis: We’ll take X-rays to assess the tooth’s condition.
  2. Anesthesia: Local anesthesia ensures a pain-free experience.
  3. Treatment: The infected pulp is removed, and the tooth is cleaned and sealed.
  4. Restoration: A crown or filling is placed to protect the tooth.

Our advanced techniques and caring approach make the process smooth and comfortable.

How Dental Implants Can Transform Your Smile

Dental implants are a permanent solution for missing teeth, offering both functional and aesthetic benefits:

  • Natural Look: Implants blend seamlessly with your natural teeth.
  • Durability: With proper care, they can last a lifetime.
  • Improved Function: Chew and speak with confidence.
  • Bone Health: Implants prevent bone loss in the jaw.

At OAK Dental Hospital, our implant specialists ensure a personalized treatment plan for the best results.
