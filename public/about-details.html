Our Journey

Since opening our doors in 2010, OAK Dental Clinic has been dedicated to transforming smiles in Hyderabad. We started as a small practice with a vision to make dental care accessible and advanced. In 2015, we introduced laser dentistry, followed by a major facility upgrade in 2018 to include digital imaging and 3D scanning. Today, we serve over 5,000 patients annually, with a focus on community outreach, including free dental camps in rural Telangana.

Our Technology

OAK Dental leverages cutting-edge technology to deliver precise and comfortable treatments. Our clinic is equipped with digital X-rays, intraoral cameras, CAD/CAM for same-day crowns, and laser dentistry for minimally invasive procedures. We also use AI-driven diagnostics to enhance treatment planning, ensuring the best outcomes for our patients.
