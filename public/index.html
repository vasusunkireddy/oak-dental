Frequently Asked Questions

1. What should I do if I have a dental emergency?

Contact OAK Dental immediately for urgent situations like severe pain or broken teeth. We’ll guide you and ensure prompt care.

2. How do I know if I need a root canal?

Signs include persistent pain, sensitivity, or gum swelling. Our specialists can assess if a root canal is necessary.

3. Are dental implants a good option for missing teeth?

Yes, implants are durable and natural-looking. They maintain jawbone health, and we can determine if they’re right for you.

4. How can I improve the appearance of my smile?

We offer teeth whitening, veneers, and orthodontics. Schedule a consultation for personalized smile enhancement options.
