About Oak Dental Clinic

Oak Dental Clinic, located in the heart of Bachupally, Hyderabad, is a premier multi-speciality and implant center dedicated to providing exceptional dental care. Established with a mission to transform smiles, our clinic combines advanced technology with a patient-centric approach to deliver outstanding results.

Our clinic is led by Dr. A. Ram Koti Reddy, a highly skilled Prosthodontist and Implantologist with over a decade of experience. Dr. Reddy holds BDS and MDS degrees and is registered under Reg. No: A19677. His expertise in prosthodontics and implantology ensures that our patients receive the highest standard of care for complex dental procedures.

At Oak Dental Clinic, we offer a comprehensive range of services, including general dentistry, cosmetic dentistry, orthodontics, and advanced implantology. Our state-of-the-art facilities are equipped with the latest dental technology, ensuring precise diagnoses and effective treatments. We prioritize patient comfort, using modern techniques to make your visit as pleasant as possible.

We believe in building lasting relationships with our patients. Our team takes the time to understand your needs, providing personalized treatment plans tailored to your oral health goals. From routine check-ups to smile makeovers, we are committed to helping you achieve a healthy, radiant smile.

Our clinic operates from the 2nd Floor, Vision Apartments, Saree Nagar, Nazampet Road, Bachupally, Hyderabad, 500090. We are open Monday to Saturday, from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM, making it convenient for you to schedule your visits. Contact us at 756 936 767 or 9502 030 637 to book your appointment today.
